Product Name: Automatic Calculation Tension Controller
Product Model: KTC818A

  • Features
  • Specifications
  • Installation
  • Manual

KTC818A Tension Control is of high accuracy, multi-function and full-digital intelligence. It can measure coiled materials’ radius by various methods, perform the tension calculation automatically and the open-loop tension control.

KTC818-1AD Tension Control adopts the brand-new graphic LCD with more clear Chinese/English display. It can send out the 0~24V DC controlling voltage instruction aiming at the magnetic clutch/brake, or output the 0~10V voltage signal to control the transducer, servo motor or other executive bodies so as to perform the high-accurate tension control for the coiled materials. It is widely used in the industries of printing, packing, tag, papermaking, plastic, cable, rubber, electronic battery, textile, printing and dyeing, etc.

1.adopt the 32-bit fast CPU, sketches liquid crystal display, humanized design and easy operation

2.support the thickness summation, just need one proximity switch or encoder but have to input materials’ thickness

3.support the ratio method, need two proximity switches without inputting materials’ thickness

4.support the ultrasonic detector, need no setting after calibrate the detector

5.double-position switch

6.24V/4A two-output, can drive the two-way brake/clutch directly

7.can output the 0-10V，4-20mA servo drive/ servo motor

8.easy installation and trial run, the process of tension calibrating is simple

9.support the Standard Modbus Communication Protocol, open up all the parameters, easy connection to PLC/touch screen

10.protect the password parameters, avoids misoperation
